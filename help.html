This tool organizes the Miami University Grade distrobution PDF files displayed at the registration site.

Fill in whatever sections you can, and leave the rest blank. (I.E. only enter ISA as DEPT). When you hit search the page will load the results (max 500).

You can click on the courses to add them to the comparison bar at the bottom (You can perform additional searches and the list won't be altered). When you want to remove the class either click the box in the search results, or click on it in the comparison bar

If you notice any discrepancies please email us @ MUGradeDist [AT] gmail.com Some issues might be:

  • Bugs
  • Inaccurate data (ie missing data)
  • Suggestions (ie new graph or feature)
  • Teacher name variance (ie John Smith and Smith, John should be merged to one instructor)
